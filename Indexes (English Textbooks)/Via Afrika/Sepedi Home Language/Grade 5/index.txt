--- TABLE OF CONTENTS ---

Term 1
  Listening and Speaking
    - Listens to stories, poems and information texts
    - Discusses and responds to texts
    - Retells stories in own words
    - Gives short prepared talks
  Reading and Viewing
    - Reads fiction and non-fiction texts
    - Uses reading strategies: predicting, skimming, scanning
    - Reads aloud with expression
    - Comprehension: answers questions, identifies main ideas
  Writing and Presenting
    - Writes narrative and descriptive paragraphs
    - Process writing: planning, drafting, editing
    - Handwriting practice
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Parts of speech: nouns, verbs, adjectives
    - Sentence construction: subject and predicate
    - Punctuation: full stop, comma, question mark
    - Spelling patterns and rules

Term 2
  Listening and Speaking
    - Listens to instructions and announcements
    - Participates in group discussions
    - Gives directions and instructions
    - Listens to poems and songs
  Reading and Viewing
    - Reads information texts with visual elements
    - Poetry: rhythm, rhyme, imagery
    - Uses dictionary for vocabulary development
    - Reading comprehension strategies
  Writing and Presenting
    - Writes instructions and directions
    - Friendly letters
    - Process writing
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Tenses: present, past, future
    - Singular and plural forms
    - Synonyms and antonyms
    - Spelling and vocabulary building

Term 3
  Listening and Speaking
    - Listens to dialogues and conversations
    - Role-play and dramatisation
    - Participates in debates (basic)
    - Oral presentations
  Reading and Viewing
    - Reads drama and dialogue texts
    - Reads for information: scanning and intensive reading
    - Visual literacy: posters, advertisements
    - Novel or story study
  Writing and Presenting
    - Writes dialogues and short plays
    - Report writing
    - Process writing
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Direct and indirect speech
    - Conjunctions and connecting words
    - Idioms and proverbs
    - Word meaning in context

Term 4
  Listening and Speaking
    - Listens to and discusses news and current events
    - Prepared and unprepared speeches
    - Listening comprehension assessment
  Reading and Viewing
    - Reads newspaper and magazine articles
    - Poetry appreciation
    - Reading comprehension revision
  Writing and Presenting
    - Summary writing
    - Transactional writing: letters, invitations
    - Process writing revision
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Revision of grammar and language structures
    - Vocabulary enrichment
    - Spelling and punctuation revision
