Play! Mathematics - Grade 4 (CAPS) - 2nd Edition, Revised and Expanded
Publisher: JNM Publishers
Authors: Mouton and Bezuidenhout

Term 1:
- Whole numbers: Counting, ordering, comparing and representing numbers (up to 4-digit numbers)
- Whole numbers: Addition and subtraction (up to 4-digit numbers)
- Number sentences
- Numeric patterns
- Whole numbers: Multiplication (2-digit by 2-digit)
- Properties of 2D shapes

Term 2:
- Whole numbers: Division (3-digit by 1-digit)
- Common fractions (halves, quarters, thirds, fifths, sixths, eighths)
- Whole numbers: Multiplication and division (continued)
- Measurement: Length (mm, cm, m, km, conversions)
- Measurement: Perimeter
- Properties of 3D objects

Term 3:
- Whole numbers: Addition, subtraction, multiplication and division
- Decimal fractions (tenths)
- Measurement: Capacity/volume (ml, l)
- Measurement: Mass (g, kg)
- Position and movement (angles, directions, maps)
- Geometric patterns
- Symmetry

Term 4:
- Whole numbers: All four operations (revision and extension)
- Common fractions (addition and subtraction of fractions with same denominator)
- Measurement: Area
- Measurement: Temperature
- Data handling (collect, organise, represent, analyse - bar graphs, pictographs)
- Numeric patterns (revision)
- Transformations
- Probability

Source: CAPS Mathematics Intermediate Phase curriculum (education.gov.za). Play! Mathematics follows CAPS in prescribed order.
