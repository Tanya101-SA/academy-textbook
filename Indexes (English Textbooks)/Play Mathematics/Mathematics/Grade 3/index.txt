Play! Mathematics - Grade 3 (CAPS)
Publisher: JNM Publishers
Authors: Mouton and Bezuidenhout

Term 1:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-1000)
- Number symbols and number names (1-1000)
- Place value (hundreds, tens, units)
- Describe, compare and order numbers to 999
- Addition and subtraction (up to 999)
- Number sentences
- Repeated addition leading to multiplication (2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 10x)
- Money: Solving problems involving money
- Time: Analogue clocks, calendars
- Properties of 2D shapes
- Data handling: Collect and organise data

Term 2:
- Whole numbers: Counting in groups (2s, 3s, 4s, 5s, 10s, 20s, 25s, 50s, 100s)
- Number patterns
- Addition and subtraction (3-digit numbers)
- Multiplication (basic facts)
- Division (basic facts with remainders)
- Fractions: Halves, quarters, thirds, fifths, sixths, eighths
- Length: mm, cm, m, km
- Mass: g, kg
- 3D objects: Recognise and describe
- Geometric patterns

Term 3:
- Whole numbers: Addition and subtraction (3-digit numbers)
- Multiplication (2-digit by 1-digit)
- Division (2-digit by 1-digit)
- Common fractions (comparing, ordering)
- Capacity: ml, l
- Temperature
- Perimeter
- Position and directions
- Symmetry
- Views of objects

Term 4:
- Whole numbers: All four operations (revision and extension)
- Number patterns
- Fractions (revision)
- Time: Read time in hours, half hours, quarter hours, minutes
- Area (informal)
- Data handling: Represent and analyse data (bar graphs, pictographs)
- Probability (certain, uncertain, impossible)
- Transformations (slides, flips, turns)

Source: CAPS Mathematics Foundation Phase curriculum (education.gov.za). Play! Mathematics follows CAPS in prescribed order.
