Play! Mathematics - Grade 7 (CAPS)
Publisher: JNM Publishers
Authors: Mouton and Bezuidenhout
ISBN: 9781928432067

Term 1:
- Whole numbers: Properties of numbers, ordering, comparing, place value (up to 9-digit numbers)
- Whole numbers: Addition, subtraction, multiplication and division
- Exponents (including squares, cubes, square roots, cube roots)
- Integers (introduction: counting, ordering, comparing, addition and subtraction)
- Common fractions (proper, improper, mixed, equivalent, addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
- Decimal fractions (ordering, place value, all four operations, rounding)

Term 2:
- Numeric and geometric patterns (investigate and extend patterns, input/output, rules)
- Functions and relationships
- Algebraic expressions (introduction)
- Algebraic equations (solving by inspection)
- Graphs (interpreting and drawing)
- Geometry of 2D shapes (classifying triangles and quadrilaterals, angle properties)
- Transformation geometry (translations, reflections, rotations, enlargements)

Term 3:
- Geometry of 3D objects (recognise, classify, describe, nets)
- Measurement: Area and perimeter of regular and irregular shapes
- Measurement: Surface area and volume of rectangular prisms
- Measurement: Conversions (length, mass, capacity, time)
- Ratio and rate
- Percentages (including percentage increase/decrease)
- Financial mathematics (profit, loss, discount, VAT)

Term 4:
- Integers (revision and extension)
- Data handling: Collect, organise, summarise and represent data (bar graphs, histograms, pie charts)
- Data handling: Interpret and analyse data (mean, median, mode)
- Probability (experiments, relative frequency)
- Numeric and geometric patterns (revision)
- Whole numbers: All four operations (revision)
- Equations (revision)

Source: CAPS Mathematics Senior Phase curriculum (education.gov.za). Play! Mathematics follows CAPS in prescribed order.
