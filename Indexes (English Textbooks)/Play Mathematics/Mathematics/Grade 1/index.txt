Play! Mathematics - Grade 1 (CAPS)
Publisher: JNM Publishers
Authors: Mouton and Bezuidenhout

Term 1:
- Whole numbers: Counting objects (1-10)
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-20)
- Number symbols and number names (1-10)
- Describe, compare and order numbers (1-10)
- Addition and subtraction (1-10)
- Repeating patterns
- Geometric patterns
- 3D objects: Recognise and name (balls, boxes)
- 2D shapes: Recognise and name (circles, triangles, squares)
- Symmetry
- Data handling: Collect and sort objects

Term 2:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-50)
- Number symbols and number names (1-25)
- Describe, compare and order numbers (1-15)
- Place value (1-15)
- Addition and subtraction (1-15)
- Money
- Time: Ordering of regular events, days of the week
- Length: Informal measuring
- 2D shapes and 3D objects
- Data handling: Represent data (pictograph)

Term 3:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-75)
- Number symbols and number names (1-50)
- Describe, compare and order numbers (1-20)
- Addition and subtraction (1-20)
- Repeated addition leading to multiplication
- Grouping and sharing leading to division
- Mass: Informal measuring (lighter, heavier)
- Capacity: Informal measuring (more, less)
- Position and directions (left, right, in front, behind)
- Geometric patterns

Term 4:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-100)
- Number symbols and number names (1-100)
- Ordinal numbers
- Addition and subtraction (1-20)
- Number patterns
- Fractions: Halves
- Time: Hours, calendars
- Length: Informal measuring
- Data handling: Analyse and interpret data
- 3D objects and 2D shapes (revision)

Source: CAPS Mathematics Foundation Phase curriculum (education.gov.za). Play! Mathematics follows CAPS in prescribed order.
