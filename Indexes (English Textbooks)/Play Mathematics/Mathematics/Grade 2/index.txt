Play! Mathematics - Grade 2 (CAPS)
Publisher: JNM Publishers
Authors: Mouton and Bezuidenhout

Term 1:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-100)
- Number symbols and number names (1-100)
- Describe, compare and order numbers to 99
- Place value (tens and units)
- Addition and subtraction (up to 99)
- Number sentences
- Repeated addition leading to multiplication
- Money: Recognising SA coins and notes
- Time: Months of the year, days of the week, hours
- 3D objects and 2D shapes
- Data handling: Collect and organise data

Term 2:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards in 2s, 5s, 10s (1-200)
- Number patterns
- Addition and subtraction (up to 99)
- Grouping and sharing (division)
- Fractions: Halves and quarters
- Length: Informal and formal measuring (cm, m)
- Symmetry
- Geometric patterns
- Data handling: Pictographs and bar graphs

Term 3:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-200)
- Addition and subtraction with carrying/borrowing
- Multiplication (2x, 5x, 10x tables)
- Division
- Mass: Grams and kilograms
- Capacity: Litres and millilitres
- Position and movement
- 3D objects and 2D shapes
- Patterns

Term 4:
- Whole numbers: Count forwards and backwards (1-200)
- Place value (hundreds, tens, units)
- Addition and subtraction (3-digit numbers)
- Multiplication and division
- Fractions: Halves, quarters, thirds
- Time: Half hours, quarter hours
- Perimeter (informal)
- Data handling: Analyse data
- Number patterns (revision)

Source: CAPS Mathematics Foundation Phase curriculum (education.gov.za). Play! Mathematics follows CAPS in prescribed order.
