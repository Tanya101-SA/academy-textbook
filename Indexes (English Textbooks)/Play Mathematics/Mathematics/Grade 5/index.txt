Play! Mathematics - Grade 5 (CAPS) - 2nd Edition, Revised and Expanded
Publisher: JNM Publishers
Authors: Mouton and Bezuidenhout

Term 1:
- Whole numbers: Counting, ordering, comparing and place value (up to 6-digit numbers)
- Whole numbers: Addition and subtraction (up to 6-digit numbers)
- Number sentences
- Numeric patterns (including rules)
- Whole numbers: Multiplication (3-digit by 2-digit)
- Properties of 2D shapes
- Whole numbers: Division (3-digit by 2-digit)

Term 2:
- Common fractions (including mixed numbers, equivalent fractions)
- Addition and subtraction of common fractions
- Whole numbers: Multiplication and division (continued)
- Measurement: Length (conversions between mm, cm, m, km)
- Measurement: Perimeter
- Properties of 3D objects
- Capacity/volume and Mass

Term 3:
- Whole numbers: All four operations (extended)
- Decimal fractions (tenths and hundredths)
- Measurement: Capacity/volume (conversions ml, l, kl)
- Measurement: Mass (conversions g, kg)
- Position and movement (grids, coordinates)
- Geometric patterns
- Symmetry
- Transformations (reflection, translation, rotation)

Term 4:
- Whole numbers: All four operations (revision)
- Common fractions (revision and extension)
- Decimal fractions (addition and subtraction)
- Measurement: Area (counting squares, rectangles)
- Measurement: Temperature
- Data handling (collect, organise, represent, analyse - bar graphs, pie charts)
- Numeric patterns (revision)
- Probability

Source: CAPS Mathematics Intermediate Phase curriculum (education.gov.za). Play! Mathematics follows CAPS in prescribed order.
