Play! Mathematics - Grade 6 (CAPS)
Publisher: JNM Publishers
Authors: Mouton and Bezuidenhout

Term 1:
- Whole numbers: Counting, ordering, comparing, representing and place value (up to 9-digit numbers)
- Whole numbers: Addition and subtraction (up to 9-digit numbers)
- Number sentences
- Numeric patterns
- Whole numbers: Multiplication (4-digit by 3-digit)
- Whole numbers: Division (4-digit by 3-digit)
- Exponents (squares and cubes)
- Properties of 2D shapes

Term 2:
- Common fractions (proper fractions, improper fractions, mixed numbers, equivalent fractions)
- Addition and subtraction of common fractions (including unlike denominators)
- Decimal fractions (to thousandths)
- Measurement: Length (conversions)
- Measurement: Perimeter
- Properties of 3D objects
- Capacity/volume and Mass

Term 3:
- Whole numbers: All four operations (extended)
- Decimal fractions (addition, subtraction, multiplication and division)
- Percentages
- Input and output (number machines)
- Measurement: Capacity/volume (conversions)
- Measurement: Mass (conversions)
- Position and movement (transformation geometry)
- Geometric patterns
- Symmetry

Term 4:
- Whole numbers: All four operations (revision)
- Common fractions, decimal fractions and percentages (relationships)
- Measurement: Area and perimeter of rectangles and squares
- Measurement: Temperature
- The history of measurement
- Data handling (collect, organise, represent, analyse - bar graphs, pie charts, histograms)
- Probability
- Numeric patterns (revision)
- Ratio and rate (introduction)

Source: CAPS Mathematics Intermediate Phase curriculum (education.gov.za). Play! Mathematics follows CAPS in prescribed order.
