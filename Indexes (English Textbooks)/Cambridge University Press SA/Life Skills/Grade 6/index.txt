      - Classification of animals and plants
    Technology
      - Structures: combining materials for strength
      - Investigating frame and shell structures
      - Designing and making a model structure for a specific purpose
  Creative Arts
    Performing Arts
      - Warming up: advanced body and vocal preparation
      - Musical elements: tempo, dynamics, timbre and texture
      - Creative dance: exploring identity and culture through movement
      - Drama: developing a character with depth and motivation
      - South African music history: from traditional to contemporary
    Visual Arts
      - Principles of design: unity, rhythm, proportion
      - Drawing: figure drawing and facial expressions
      - Cultural art: exploring South African art traditions (Ndebele, Zulu, San)
      - Painting: expressing themes of identity and diversity
  Physical Education
    - Locomotor and manipulative skill combinations
    - Athletics: sprinting technique, relay changeovers, pacing
    - Invasion games: netball (positional play, tactics, mini-matches)
    - Fitness: cardiovascular, muscular strength and endurance
    - Warm-up and cool-down principles
    - Goal setting for personal fitness
