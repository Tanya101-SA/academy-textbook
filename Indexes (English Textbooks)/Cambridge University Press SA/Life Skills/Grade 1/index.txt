--- TABLE OF CONTENTS ---

Term 1
  Beginning Knowledge
    Social Sciences
      - Me: Who am I?
      - My family and my home
      - My school and classroom
    Natural Sciences
      - My senses (sight, hearing, smell, taste, touch)
      - My body parts and what they do
    Technology
      - Structures around me (things that stand up)
      - Making a simple structure using recyclable materials
  Personal and Social Well-being
    - All about me: my name, my age, what I like
    - My feelings (happy, sad, angry, scared)
    - Manners and respect at school
    - My family members and their roles
    - Getting along with others
  Creative Arts
    Performing Arts
      - Action songs and movement to music
      - Rhythmic patterns using body percussion
      - Creative movement: imitating animals
      - Simple drama: role-play of family scenes
    Visual Arts
      - Drawing myself and my family
      - Working with colour: primary colours
      - Cutting and pasting to make a collage
      - Modelling with clay or playdough
  Physical Education
    - Locomotor movements: walking, running, hopping, skipping
    - Spatial awareness: moving in different directions
    - Balance activities: standing on one foot
    - Playing simple games with rules
    - Safety in the play area

Term 2
  Beginning Knowledge
    Social Sciences
      - My school: people who help us at school
      - Rules at school and at home
      - Special days and celebrations
    Natural Sciences
      - Weather: sunny, cloudy, rainy, windy
      - Observing weather changes each day
    Technology
      - Moving toys: wheels and axles
      - Making a simple moving toy
  Personal and Social Well-being
    - Healthy habits: washing hands, brushing teeth
    - Keeping my body clean
    - Healthy food choices
    - Road safety: crossing the road safely
    - Stranger danger awareness
  Creative Arts
    Performing Arts
      - Singing familiar songs and rhymes
      - Clapping rhythms and keeping a beat
      - Creative movement: weather and seasons
      - Puppet play and storytelling
    Visual Arts
      - Painting with different colours
      - Printing with natural objects (leaves, vegetables)
      - Drawing animals and nature
      - Creating patterns and shapes
  Physical Education
    - Ball skills: rolling, throwing, catching
    - Jumping and landing safely
    - Climbing and hanging activities
    - Cooperative games with a partner
    - Following instructions in movement activities

Term 3
  Beginning Knowledge
    Social Sciences
      - My home and different types of homes
      - Helpers in the community (doctor, nurse, police, firefighter)
      - Transport: how we travel
    Natural Sciences
      - Animals around us: pets and farm animals
      - What animals need to live (food, water, shelter)
    Technology
      - Shelters: structures that protect us
      - Making a simple shelter for an animal
  Personal and Social Well-being
    - Dealing with feelings: when I am sad or angry
    - Being kind and sharing with others
    - My responsibilities at home and at school
    - People who care for me
    - Saying no to things that can harm me
  Creative Arts
    Performing Arts
      - Action rhymes and finger plays
      - Musical instruments: shakers and drums
      - Drama: acting out community helpers
      - Movement sequences: start, middle, end
    Visual Arts
      - Constructing with boxes and containers (3D art)
      - Drawing and colouring different homes
      - Weaving with paper strips
      - Creating art from natural materials
  Physical Education
    - Relay races and running games
    - Kicking a ball and dribbling
    - Gymnastics: rolling and tumbling
    - Obstacle courses
    - Games that develop coordination

Term 4
  Beginning Knowledge
    Social Sciences
      - Road safety signs and rules
      - Keeping safe at home and at school
      - Clean and dirty environments
    Natural Sciences
      - Plants around us: parts of a plant
      - What plants need to grow (water, sunlight, soil)
      - Growing a bean seed
    Technology
      - Planting and growing: simple gardening tools
      - Making a container for a plant
  Personal and Social Well-being
    - What I have learnt this year
    - How I have grown and changed
    - Setting goals for next year
    - Celebrating our achievements
    - Being thankful for family and friends
  Creative Arts
    Performing Arts
      - Preparing a short performance for an audience
      - Singing songs about nature and plants
      - Creative dance: growing like a plant
      - Drama: acting out a favourite story
    Visual Arts
      - Drawing and painting plants and flowers
      - Making a greeting card
      - Collage using leaves, seeds and flowers
      - Decorating a frame or border
  Physical Education
    - Sports day activities: races and relays
    - Skipping with a rope
    - Balancing on different body parts
    - Swimming safety awareness
    - Fun fitness activities and cool-down exercises
