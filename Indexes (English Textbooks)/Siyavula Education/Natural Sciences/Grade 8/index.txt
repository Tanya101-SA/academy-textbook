      - Respiration (the process, word equation)
      - Aerobic respiration in cells
      - The relationship between photosynthesis and respiration
      - Investigating photosynthesis (testing leaves for starch, effect of light)

    Topic: Interactions and interdependence within the environment
      - Ecosystems (biotic and abiotic components)

      - Energy flow through an ecosystem

      - Competition (inter-specific and intra-specific)
      - Adaptations of organisms to their environment
      - Balance in an ecosystem

      - Antibiotics and antifungals
      - HIV and AIDS
      - Preserving food from micro-organisms

    Topic: Static electricity

      - Charging by friction (triboelectric effect)
      - Attraction and repulsion of charges
      - Charging by contact and induction

      - Electric current (flow of charge)
      - Potential difference (voltage)
      - Resistance

      - What is visible light?
      - Light travels in straight lines (ray model)
      - Reflection of light (laws of reflection, plane mirrors)
      - Refraction of light (bending of light, lenses)
