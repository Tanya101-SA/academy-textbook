Kusa Kusa - Grade 11 IsiZulu First Additional Language (Prescribed Novel)
Author: S.P. Nhleko
Publisher: Shuter and Shooter

Note: This is a prescribed literature novel. The novel follows the life of Thabani, a young man who grows up in a rural village in South Africa. His life is marked by tragedy and hardship, being orphaned at a young age and navigating challenges of poverty and societal expectations.

Themes: Resilience, hope, impact of societal inequalities, identity, growing up, rural life
