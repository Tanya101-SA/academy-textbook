Elokufa Alitsheli - Grade 10 IsiZulu First Additional Language (Prescribed Novel)
Author: M.M. Ndlovu (Mandla Ndlovu)
Publisher: Shuter and Shooter

Note: This is a prescribed literature novel, not a traditional textbook. Below are the chapters (izahluko):

Isahluko 1: Ukufika kukaVela ekhaya likaNgwane (Vela's arrival at Ngwane's homestead)
Isahluko 2: Ukuphila kukaVela noMahiake noKhethiwe (Vela's life with Mahiake and Khethiwe)
Isahluko 3: Izinkinga zasekhaya (Family problems and conflict)
Isahluko 4: Ubungane phakathi kukaVela noBhiza (Friendship between Vela and Bhiza)
Isahluko 5: Ingcindezi yabasebasha (Peer pressure and challenges)
Isahluko 6: Udlame nokuhlukumezana (Violence and abuse)
Isahluko 7: Ukuzama ukuthola isisombululo (Seeking solutions)
Isahluko 8: Isiphetho (The ending/conclusion)

Themes: Family conflict, peer pressure, violence, resilience, growing up
