--- TABLE OF CONTENTS ---

Term 1
  Listening and Speaking
    - Group discussion/conversation: listen to/watch an advertisement
    - Tone, pacing, emotive and manipulative language use
    - Listening comprehension: advertisement
    - Identify persuasive/manipulative techniques
  Reading and Viewing
    - Reading/viewing for comprehension: visual text (advertisement, poster, cartoons, comic strips)
    - Skimming, scanning, intensive reading, inferences
    - Emotive language, body language, use of punctuation and font
    - Literary text: youth novel/short stories/drama
    - Reading strategies: summarising, visualizing, inferring meaning
  Writing and Presenting
    - Transactional texts: advertisement/poster
    - Correct format, purpose, text features, language use, register
    - Process writing: planning, drafting, revision, editing, proof-reading
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Word level: spelling and spelling patterns, abbreviations
    - Sentence level: sentence structure, nouns, adjectives, pronouns, concord, simple tenses
    - Vocabulary in context

Term 2
  Listening and Speaking
    - Listening for comprehension: listen to a story
    - Forum/group discussion: culture
    - Roles, turn-taking, viewpoints, consensus, appropriate language and register
  Reading and Viewing
    - Literature text: drama/short story/folklore/novel
    - Plot, character, setting, narrator, mood, theme, narrator's perspective
    - Poetry: figures of speech, imagery, rhyme, rhythm, stanzas, typography
    - Figurative meaning, mood, theme and message
  Writing and Presenting
    - Transactional texts: diary entry/constitution/policy
    - Correct format, purpose, main and supporting ideas, register
    - Process writing: planning, drafting, revision, editing, proof-reading
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Word level: stems, prefixes and suffixes, pronouns
    - Sentence level: sentence types, generalisations, direct/indirect speech, voice, tenses
    - Word meaning: idioms and proverbs, literal and figurative meaning
    - Punctuation and spelling: quotation marks, abbreviations

Term 3
  Listening and Speaking
    - Dialogue/negotiations: listen to a negotiation scene
    - Structure and development of ideas, negotiation skills
    - Discussion based on understanding a will and testament
  Reading and Viewing
    - Read an abridged will and testament
    - Role players, date, possessions, beneficiaries, language of the document
    - Poetry: figures of speech, imagery, rhyme, rhythm, stanzas
    - Figurative meaning, mood, theme and message
  Writing and Presenting
    - Transactional text: will and testament
    - Correct format, purpose, main and supporting ideas, register
    - Process writing: planning, drafting, revision, editing, proof-reading
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Word level: concrete and abstract nouns, adjectives, conjunctions and transitions
    - Sentence level: speech, tenses, sentence types, paragraph types, voice, clauses and phrases
    - Word meaning: paronyms, polysemes, homonyms, homophones
    - Punctuation and spelling patterns

Term 4
  Listening and Speaking
    - Listening comprehension: reacts critically on a variety of texts
    - Listen for specific information, enjoy fables and tales
    - Meeting procedure: role players, conventions, turn-taking, language use
  Reading and Viewing
    - Literary text: short story, youth novel/novel
    - Key features: character, action, dialogue, plot, conflict, setting, narrator, theme
    - Poetry: figures of speech, imagery, rhyme, rhythm, stanzas
    - Reading comprehension: skimming, scanning, visualization, inference, fact and opinion
  Writing and Presenting
    - Long transactional text: letter of application (formal)
    - Correct format, purpose, main and supporting ideas, cohesion
    - Process writing: planning, drafting, revision, editing, proof-reading
  Language Structures and Conventions
    - Word level: verbs
    - Sentence level: direct and indirect speech, active and passive voice
    - Word meaning: ambiguity, cliche, redundancy, tautology, slang, jargon
    - Punctuation and spelling patterns
