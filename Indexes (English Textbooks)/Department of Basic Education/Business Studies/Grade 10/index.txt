DBE Business Studies - Grade 10 Learners Book (CAPS)
Publisher: Department of Basic Education (South Africa)

BUSINESS ENVIRONMENTS
- Micro environment (Components: vision, mission, business objectives, resources, management, functions)
- Market environment (Suppliers, customers, competitors, intermediaries, regulators)
- Macro environment (Political/legal, economic, social, technological, physical/environmental, international)
- Business sectors (Primary, secondary, tertiary sectors and their interrelationships)
- Contemporary socio-economic issues (HIV/AIDS, poverty, inequality, unemployment)

BUSINESS OPERATIONS
- Sole traders
- Partnerships
- Close corporations
- Companies (Private and public)
- Franchises
- Co-operatives

BUSINESS VENTURES
- Entrepreneurship
- Starting a business (Business plan, location, financing)

BUSINESS ROLES
- Creative thinking and problem solving
- Relationships and team performance
